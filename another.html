"Director Jiang, Director Xu sent someone to deliver two pairs of ten carat earrings. He wants to cooperate with you. " The assistant said. Vulgar, throw them away. " Jiang Xiaorou stared at the assistant. "In the future, whoever sends cars, houses, and diamond rings will be told to scram. Do I look like someone who lacks money?"
